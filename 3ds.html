Overview

Welcome to ADC Mission 2026: Time Warp

Competition Format
Teams of 2-4 students compete in teamwork challenges and individual skills challenges. Each match features two alliances working together to score points by navigating through time portals and collecting temporal artifacts.
Key Objectives
  • Navigate drone through time portals
  • Collect temporal artifacts
  • Score in designated zones
  • Complete precision maneuvers

Rules Summary

General Rules
  • G1: Read and know the rules
  • G2: Be respectful at all times
  • G3: Robots must pass inspection before competing
  • G4: Teams must be present for all scheduled matches
  • G5: Safety is paramount - unsafe drones will be disqualified
Robot Specifications
Spec Requirement
Max Size 12" diameter
Weight No limit
Battery LiPo 3S max
Props Safety guards required

Mission Details

Match Format
Each match is 2 minutes long with two phases:
  • Autonomous (30 sec): Pre-programmed flight
  • Driver Control (90 sec): Manual piloting
Field Elements
  • Time Portals (4 locations)
  • Artifact Collection Zones (3 zones)
  • Temporal Gates (precision flying)
  • Landing Pads (bonus points)

Scoring Guide

Point Values
Action Points
Portal Navigation 5 pts each
Artifact Collection 3 pts each
Gate Precision Flight 10 pts
Landing Pad (Auto) 15 pts
Landing Pad (Driver) 10 pts
Autonomous Bonus
Complete any scoring action during autonomous period for 2x points!

Skills Challenge

Programming Skills
60 seconds of autonomous flight. Program your drone to complete as many scoring actions as possible without driver input.
Driver Skills
60 seconds of driver-controlled flight. Demonstrate precision piloting by completing the course as quickly and accurately as possible.
